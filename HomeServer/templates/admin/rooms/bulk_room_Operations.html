Action Reference

  • Release to VacantRemoves active member allocations. Device mappings are preserved — slots go INACTIVE and return to the vacant pool.

  • DeactivateReleases all active members from the room and forces Room.status → VACANT. Use when taking a room out of rotation entirely.

  • ActivateMarks Room.status → VACANT so it appears in the allocation pool. No members are touched.

  • Set TypeUpdates Room.room_type only. Does not move or remove any allocations — existing members retain their current rows.

Destructive actions cannot be undone. "Release to Vacant" and "Deactivate" immediately remove member access. Double-check your room selection before submitting.
